The compliance promise

If a payroll provider offers you money back, walk away. Here's why.

⚠️ "4% of net payroll back, tax free" is not a scheme — it's fraud.

Outsourcing payroll doesn't change the PAYE and National Insurance that's legally due, so there is no legitimate saving to rebate. Providers making these offers typically operate mini umbrella company fraud — fragmenting workers into small shell companies to abuse the Employment Allowance and VAT Flat Rate Scheme. HMRC has deregistered tens of thousands of these companies and a 2025 Upper Tribunal ruling confirmed the model is fraudulent.

Worse for you: since 6 April 2026, joint and several liability rules mean the agency or end client is on the hook for an umbrella's unpaid PAYE — with no reasonable-care defence. If your provider doesn't pay, HMRC comes to you.

PayBridge never touches your tax money. You pay HMRC, your pension provider and your employees directly from your own account. We calculate, file and report — every payment is visible to you, every filing is under your own PAYE scheme.
